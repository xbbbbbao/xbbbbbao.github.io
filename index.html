Calling for Satellite Partners
Front your CSR efforts with Hair for Hope 2021

After a one-year hiatus, our flagship fundraiser Hair for Hope (HfH) is back – and it’s VIRTUAL! Hair for Hope (HfH) is Children’s Cancer Foundation’s (CCF) flagship fundraiser – the only headshaving campaign in Singapore that serves to raise childhood cancer awareness and funds.

We understand that the pandemic has brought about unprecedented challenges that may have affected your organisation’s efforts to engage in social responsibility. This year, we bring HfH to you in a new format with minimal coordination and greater convenience so you can continue to conduct CSR and virtual team bonding activities safely.

For enquiries or to register your interest, drop us a note at info@hairforhope.org.sg or simply click ‘Reply’ to this email.

Together, let us tell children with cancer and their families that it is OK to be bald, and they are not alone.

In its 18th year running, Hair for Hope is Children’s Cancer Foundation’s flagship fundraiser – the only head-shaving campaign in Singapore that serves to raise funds and awareness of childhood cancer. Every shaven head at Hair for Hope represents the understanding by an individual of the ordeals that a child with cancer is subjected to. For the latest campaign happenings, follow us on HfH Facebook/ CCF Instagram.
